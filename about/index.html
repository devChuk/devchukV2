ABOUT

They call me Chuk
I'm a New York native, StuyCS Alumnus, and hackNY Fellow studying Computer Science at New York University.

I am a developer evangelist for DaaS platform Syncano and previously a software engineering intern at healthcare startup Betterpath. I also do freelance jobs in my spare time.

I got my start in programming by building drones & robotic arms from scratch in high school. My experience enabled me to develop robotic arms for the NYU Mechatronics Research Lab and drones for Kite & Rocket, the product-development firm behind the Nerf gun lines.

I'm a maker, a tinkerer, and a hacker. I've travelled to several hackathons and worked on a ton of projects, from wooden bikes to Android apps. I strongly believe in learning by doing and taking education into your own hands.
